# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-12_

**termcast** ("the App") is a personal automation tool that generates short
educational videos explaining IT and business terms and publishes them to the
operator's own social media accounts (including TikTok via the TikTok Content
Posting API).

## Information We Handle

- **TikTok account credentials (OAuth tokens):** The App stores the access token
  and refresh token issued by TikTok solely to publish videos to the account that
  authorized the App. These tokens are kept locally in the operator's own
  environment and are never shared with third parties.
- **Generated content:** Video files, captions, and term data produced by the App.
- The App does **not** collect, store, or process personal data of any TikTok
  viewers or other end users.

## How We Use Information

The OAuth tokens are used only to call the TikTok Content Posting API on behalf of
the account owner in order to upload and/or post the App's own generated videos.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Data is transmitted
only to TikTok's official API endpoints as required to perform uploads.

## Data Retention

Tokens and generated files are retained locally only as long as needed for
operation and can be deleted by the operator at any time.
